E-BIKES

Riding an e-bike brings a sense of freedom you can't get from driving a car. It lowers personal travel costs, helps to cut carbon emissions and reduces inner city travel time. In addition to this, cycling - including using an e-bike or non-standard cycle - has proven mental and physical health benefits.

An electric bicycle, e-bike, electrically assisted pedal cycle, or electrically power assisted cycle is a motorized bicycle with an integrated electric motor used to assist propulsion.
